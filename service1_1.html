Weather Tips

Rainy Days

Always carry an umbrella when rain is forecasted. Check the hourly forecast for the best time to plan outdoor activities.

Sunny Weather

Don't forget sunscreen and stay hydrated during hot days. Plan outdoor activities in the morning or evening to avoid peak heat.

Cold Weather

Layer your clothing and protect exposed skin from frostbite. Check road conditions before traveling in snowy weather.

Storm Safety

Seek shelter indoors during thunderstorms. Avoid tall objects, open fields, and bodies of water when lightning is present.
